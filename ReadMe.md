##  Overview
An interactive dashboard visualizing global trends in electric vehicle (EV) adoption between 2015 and 2023.  
The project explores EV **sales**, **stock**, and **adoption share**, highlighting the top 10 markets and the growth trajectory by powertrain type.

##  Key Visuals
- **EV Sales Trend:** Year-over-year growth in global EV sales  
- **Top 10 Countries:** Highest EV stock in 2023  
- **EV Stock Share Trend:** Global adoption momentum  
- **Powertrain Mix:** BEV vs PHEV vs FCEV breakdown  
- **EV Sales vs Stock (Scatter):** Market maturity vs growth relationship

##  Insights
- Global EV sales reached **33.1 million** in 2023.  
- Cumulative stock surpassed **99 million vehicles**.  
- **China, Europe, and the USA** dominate both stock and sales volumes.  
- BEVs represent ~70% of total 2023 EV sales.

##  Tools & Methods
- Power BI: Data modeling, DAX, visualization  
- Data Prep: Power Query for cleaning and parameter-based filtering  
- DAX Measures:Total EV Sales, Total EV Stock, Avg EV Stock Share  
- Dataset:[IEA Global EV Data 2024](https://www.iea.org/data-and-statistics/data-product/global-ev-data)
